### **Introduction to Problem Solving Technique**

**Problem solving** means finding a way to fix a problem or answer a question.

A **problem-solving technique** is the method you use to solve a problem step by step.

#### **Basic Steps:**

1. **Understand the problem**  
   Read or listen carefully. Know what is wrong or what is needed.  
2. **Think of solutions**  
   List possible ways to solve it.  
3. **Choose the best solution**  
   Pick the easiest or most effective one.  
4. **Try the solution**  
   Do the plan.  
5. **Check the result**  
   See if the problem is really solved.

#### **Example:**

Problem: You forgot your homework.  
Solutions:

* Ask teacher for extra time  
* Finish it quickly during break

Choose the best, do it, and check the result.

So, **problem-solving technique \= steps to find and fix a problem.**
